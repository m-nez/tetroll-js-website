How to play

Complete 21 lines first. Do not fill up the board 3 times.

Controls

move blocks - A, S, D, Left Arrow, Down Arrow, Right Arrow, J, K, L
rotate blocks clockwise - W, E, Up Arrow, I, O
rotate blocks counterclockwise - Q, U
drop - Shift
